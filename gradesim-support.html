Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock unlimited classes?
The free version includes one class with all features. To add unlimited classes, tap the "Upgrade to Pro" button in the app. It's a one-time purchase of $3.99 (or £3.49) — no subscription required.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to make the original purchase.
How does the Simulate feature work?
Open any class, tap "Grade Calculator," then go to the "Simulate" tab. Add hypothetical grades for upcoming assignments and see how they would affect your final grade. Your simulated grades won't affect your actual grade — you can clear them anytime.
Which grading systems does GradeSim support?
GradeSim supports:
  • US and Canadian GPA (4.0 scale)
  • US High School weighted GPA with Honors and AP/IB (5.0 scale)
  • UK University (First, 2:1, 2:2, Third)
  • UK A-Level (A* to E)
  • UK GCSE (9 to 1)
You can select your system in Settings.
How is GPA calculated?
GPA is calculated using credit-weighted averages. Each class's grade is multiplied by its credit hours, then divided by total credit hours. This ensures harder classes (with more credits) have appropriate weight in your GPA.
Can I use GradeSim on multiple devices?
GradeSim stores data locally on your device. Your data doesn't automatically sync between devices. However, your Pro purchase will restore on any device using the same Apple ID or Google account.
How do I delete a class or semester?
Long press on the class or semester you want to delete, then select "Delete" from the menu. You can also swipe left on the item to reveal the delete option.
Is my data private?
Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or have access to your grades or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Can I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google, not by us directly. To request a refund:
  • iOS: Go to reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID
  • Android: Visit the Google Play Store, find GradeSim in your purchase history, and select "Request a refund"
