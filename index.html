Privacy Policy for GradeSim

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

We don't collect your data. GradeSim stores all your information locally on your device. We don't have accounts, we don't track you, and we can't see your grades.

Information We Do NOT Collect

GradeSim does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Your grades, classes, or academic information
  • Device identifiers for advertising

Information Stored on Your Device

GradeSim stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Classes and semesters you create
  • Grades and assignments you enter
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device, is not uploaded to any server, is not accessible to us or any third party, and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat receives transaction information from Apple App Store or Google Play Store and a random anonymous identifier to track your purchase status. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any personal information from us.

For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store / Google Play Store

Purchases are processed through Apple or Google's payment systems. These transactions are governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

GradeSim does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security. We recommend using a device passcode or biometric lock and keeping your device's operating system updated.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, export, or modify. Your data is entirely in your control on your device. To delete all GradeSim data, simply uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
